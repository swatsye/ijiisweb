Services

We are IJIIS™

IJIIS was founded as an internet services center in the early 2000s. After a brief break from the business, IJIIS is revamped into a software solutions provider with services ranging from custom software development, web and mobile app design, digital marketing and SEO. We have services that can help new businesses have a virtual presence, a strategical digital transformation for existing ones and also reform older ones.

IJIIS is led by a team of professionals having over 15 years of industry experience in the fields of technology, marketing, design and customer relationship. We can help you build a brand from its conception and through its productive life. With us you're in safe hands.

Mission

Build IT solutions with awe-spiring designs and usability to enable businesses join the worldwide digital transformation bandwagon.

Vision

Create ground-breaking, sustainable software products to empower small to medium businesses to reach more customers, offer effective services and multiply turnovers..

Values

We are governed by the core principles of - Integrity, Innovation, Commitment and Passion.

Services

We specialize in the art of designing seamless software solutions for your business.

IJIIS™ is a digital consultancy that collaborates with your enterprise to create flawless end-to-end solutions with exceptional design, rich user interface and high security. We pledge to put your company on an online pedestal with our digital marketing services to ensure traffic and visibility.

Build your brand’s omnipresence with our tailored web designs and stay accessible and open for business 24/7. Let customers find you at the end of their hands. Digital Marketing & SEO

Strategize your digital marketing to build brand awareness campaigns and visibility to your business. Through our SEO services enhance your search result ranking and generate quality traffic for your website.

Content & Copywriting

Social Media Management
